My top 3 movies of all-time

96

96 is a old school love tale of a couple who is destined to be apart meets in a get together after 10 years and regrets when realizing they just missed all the oppurtunities to be together

Interstellar

Interstellar is a sci-fi master piece od Christopher nolan and is just a must watch for science lovers
